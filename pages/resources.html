Mental Health Resources

We believe in empowering our clients with knowledge and support outside of therapy sessions. Here are some trusted resources for mental health information and emergency support.

Emergency Contacts

If you or someone you know is in immediate danger, please call emergency services or go to the nearest casualty department.

  • SADAG (SA Depression & Anxiety Group): 0800 567 567
  • Suicide Crisis Line: 0800 567 567
  • Lifeline: 0861 322 322
