Psychotherapy Informed Consent

& PRACTICE AGREEMENT

Introduction & Purpose

This document provides important information about your psychotherapy services, your rights, and the terms under which Hopeful Seasons Wellness will provide psychological support. It is designed to help you make an informed decision about therapy. An informed consent form should give clients clear expectations about the process, confidentiality, risks, and limits of therapy.

Nature of Therapy

Psychotherapy is a collaborative process. It may involve discussion of feelings, thoughts, life patterns, and behaviours, and can include emotional discomfort at times. Outcomes vary for each individual no specific result or guarantee of improvement can be assured.

Therapist Background

Your therapist is a registered Clinical Psychologist trained in systems-based, client-centred therapy incorporating psychodynamic and cognitive behavioural approaches.

Confidentiality

Your information is confidential and will not be shared without your written consent except where required by law, such as if there is risk of harm to yourself or others or where required by court order or legal process.

Fees & Payments

  • Standard fee per session: R1100 (cash payment) unless otherwise agreed.
  • Medical aid claims: dependent on your plan coverage and benefits; receipts will be provided where applicable.
  • Fees for extended assessments and reports (e.g., psycho-legal) will be discussed and confirmed in writing before commencement.

Cancellation Policy

  • Please provide 24 hours’ notice for cancellations.
  • Late cancellations or missed sessions may be charged in full.

Client Rights

You have the right to:

  • Ask questions about my qualifications or therapy process.
  • Withdraw from therapy at any time.
  • Request clarification on policies or decisions.

Voluntary Participation

Participation in therapy is voluntary. You may discontinue therapy at any time without penalty.
