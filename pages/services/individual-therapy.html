Individual Therapy

Individual therapy provides a confidential and supportive environment where you can explore your thoughts, feelings, and behaviors. It is a collaborative process between you and the therapist.

What We Treat

  • Anxiety and Stress Management
  • Depression and Mood Disorders
  • Trauma and PTSD
  • Grief and Bereavement
  • Self-esteem and Personal Growth
  • Life Transitions

Our Approach

We use an integrative approach drawing from Psychodynamic Therapy and Cognitive Behavioral Therapy (CBT) to help you gain insight into your patterns and develop practical coping mechanisms.
