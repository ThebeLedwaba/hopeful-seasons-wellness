Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about therapy and our practice

How long is a typical session?

Individual sessions typically last 50-60 minutes. Initial consultations may sometimes run slightly longer to ensure a thorough understanding of your needs.

Do you accept medical aid?

Yes, we are registered with the Board of Healthcare Funders (BHF) and can claim directly from most medical aids, subject to your specific plan and available benefits.

How many sessions will I need?

The number of sessions varies depending on your individual goals and the nature of the challenges you're facing. Some clients find benefit in short-term support (6-12 sessions), while others prefer longer-term therapeutic work.

What is your cancellation policy?

We require at least 24 hours' notice for cancellations. Sessions cancelled with less than 24 hours' notice will be charged at the full rate, as that time was reserved exclusively for you.
